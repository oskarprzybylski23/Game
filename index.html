How to Play

In this game you set off on a hike. Your goal is to walk to the end point before the time runs out and the sun sets behind the horizon.

Unfortunately you forgot a map...
You only get to see the area for a few seconds before you have to find your way.

How to Play

You need to focus and remember as much of the route as you can in that time.
The finish point is marked with a signpost.

You can follow the trail, but remember, often the best route leads off the well trodden path.

How to Play

Try to find the fastest route by looking for shortcuts and exploring the area.
Do not be scared to cross the rivers and swim a bit.

Pack your lunch on go on a hike!
